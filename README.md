## What is a Bottleneck Calculator?

A Bottleneck Calculator is an online tool that helps users determine whether their computer's CPU and GPU are working together efficiently. It is designed for gamers, PC builders, content creators, streamers, programmers, and anyone planning to upgrade or optimize their computer. A hardware bottleneck occurs when one component limits the performance of another, preventing the system from reaching its full potential.
